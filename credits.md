# Credits And External Resource Attribution

This file is the canonical project-facing attribution record for reVISit-XR stimulus package. Bundled upstream metadata and source readmes are kept with the data packages for fuller provenance.

## Our World In Data Datasets

- Demo 1 and Demo 3 use local OWID life expectancy data. Bundled provenance lives in `demo1/data/life-expectancy.metadata.json`, `demo1/docs/life-expectancy-readme.md`, and matching Demo 3 copies under `demo3/data/`. Local metadata cites Riley (2005), Zijdeman et al. (2015), Human Mortality Database (2025), and United Nations World Population Prospects (2024), with major processing by Our World in Data.
- Demo 1 and Demo 3 use local OWID GDP per capita data from `gdp-per-capita-worldbank`. Bundled metadata cites Eurostat, OECD, IMF, and World Bank (2026), with minor processing by Our World in Data.
- Demo 1 and Demo 3 use local OWID CO2 emissions per capita data from `co-emissions-per-capita`. Bundled metadata cites Global Carbon Budget (2025) and population data based on various sources (2024), with major processing by Our World in Data.
- Demo 1 and Demo 3 use local OWID population data from `population-unwpp`. Bundled metadata cites United Nations World Population Prospects (2024), processed by Our World in Data.
- Demo 2 uses OWID international migrant stock data from `migrant-stock-total`. Bundled metadata cites United Nations Department of Economic and Social Affairs (2024), with minor processing by Our World in Data.
- Demo 2 uses an OWID migration-flows export for emigrants from Afghanistan. Bundled provenance in `owid-migration-flows-export-readme.md` and `owid-migration-flows-export.json` cites United Nations Department of Economic and Social Affairs (2024), with major processing by Our World in Data.
- Legacy Example 1 uses OWID per-capita primary energy data from `per-capita-energy-stacked`. Bundled provenance in `example1/data/per-capita-energy-stacked.metadata.json` and `example1/docs/per-capita-energy-stacked-readme.md` cites Energy Institute - Statistical Review of World Energy (2025) and population data based on various sources (2024), with major processing by Our World in Data.

## Geographic Boundaries

- Demo 2 bundles `demo2/data/geo/world-atlas-countries-110m.json`, derived from `world-atlas` `countries-110m.json`. The local source note in `demo2/data/geo/SOURCE.md` points to `https://unpkg.com/world-atlas@2.0.2/countries-110m.json`, Natural Earth, and Natural Earth's public-domain terms.
